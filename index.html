What is World Cup

The FIFA World Cup is the world's most prestigious and popular international soccer tournament, where national teams from across the globe compete to be crowned world champions. Held every four years by the sport's global governing body, FIFA, the event involves a lengthy qualification process, followed by a month-long final tournament in a host nation (or nations). It is a massive cultural phenomenon that brings billions of people together, transcending national borders and languages in a shared celebration of the sport. Beyond the on-field competition, the World Cup generates immense national pride, creates global storylines, and serves as a major platform for cultural exchange . The history of the world cup go back to more than a hundred of year, where the tournament begin every 4 years, to now that the next one gonna be in 2026, and will be in the US and Mexico.
